About the Podcast

Our Story

SoundWave was born from a passion for music and the stories behind the artists who create it. What started as casual conversations between friends has grown into a platform that reaches thousands of listeners every week.

Our mission is simple: to explore the diverse world of music, from chart-topping hits to underground gems, and to share the fascinating stories of the people who make the music we love.

Each episode takes you on a journey through different genres, eras, and cultures, with special guests ranging from emerging artists to industry veterans. We dive deep into creative processes, industry insights, and the personal journeys that shape musical expression.

Expect authentic conversations, surprising revelations, and plenty of musical discoveries that will expand your horizons and deepen your appreciation for the art form.

Meet The Host

maxamed sakariye

With over a decade of experience in the music industry, Alex brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to every episode of SoundWave. Starting as a radio DJ in college, Alex developed a deep appreciation for diverse musical genres and the stories behind the artists.

After graduating with a degree in Musicology, Alex worked as a music journalist for several major publications, interviewing hundreds of artists and covering music festivals worldwide. This experience provided the perfect foundation for launching SoundWave in 2018.

Alex's interviewing style is known for being insightful yet approachable, creating conversations that feel like intimate chats between friends. When not hosting the podcast, you can find Alex collecting vinyl records, attending live shows, or searching for the perfect cup of coffee.
